 Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering & Technology is an autonomous engineering institute located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 2006, it is affiliated to Anna University. As of 2019, four of its programmes are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation. Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology is in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. It offers engineering courses at UG and PG levels in civil, mechanical, and computer science engineering. It also enrolls students in interdisciplinary courses like agricultural engineering, biomedical engineering, and biotechnology. Besides this, it also offers specialization in machine learning and data science, which are current demands of industries. The institute recently became the First Engineering College to Launch own Satellite ,which reflects its quality education, infrastructure, and human resource. It is a 30-acre eco-friendly and solar energy-powered campus. Several organizations like Times of India, Higher Education, and Anna University have ranked the institute among the best in categories.
